What is Civil Engineering?

Civil Engineering is a professional discipline that deals with planning, designing, constructing, and maintaining the built environment. It involves applying mathematics, physics, and engineering principles to create structures that are strong, safe, and functional.

Civil engineers analyze materials, consider environmental factors, and use modern technology to solve problems related to infrastructure development. Their work ensures that communities have access to essential facilities such as roads, bridges, drainage systems, and public buildings.

In simple terms, civil engineering focuses on creating and improving the physical structures that support modern society.
